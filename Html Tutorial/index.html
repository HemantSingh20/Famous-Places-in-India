Delhi
Delhi, a fascinating metropolis, is both the capital of India and a National Capital Territory.
Amritsar
Amritsar, the crown jewel of Punjab, is a vibrant city that seamlessly blends spiritual significance, rich history, and cultural delights.
Patna
Patna, earlier known as Pataliputra, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited places in the world. Founded in the 6th century BCE, it flourished as the capital of mighty empires like the Mauryas and the Guptas.
Mumbai
Mumbai, the "City of Dreams," is a vibrant metropolis that pulsates with energy and offers a unique blend of history, culture, and modern life.
Hydrabad
Hyderabad, also known as the "City of Pearls," is a captivating blend of history, culture, and innovation in southern India.
Varanasi
Varanasi, also known as Kashi and Banaras, is a city in northern India revered as the spiritual capital of Hinduism. It's one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, dating back to the 11th century BCE.
Kolkata
Kolkata, formerly known as Calcutta, is a vibrant metropolis brimming with history, culture, and artistic energy.
Bengaluru
Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is a dynamic city in southern India that seamlessly blends modern innovation with a rich historical tapestry.
Jaipur
Jaipur, also known as the "Pink City," is a vibrant and historic city located in Rajasthan, India. It's famous for its rich cultural heritage, stunning palaces, and colorful bazaars.
Jammu & Kashmir
Jammu and Kashmir, a region administered by India as a union territory, is a land of breathtaking beauty and complex history.
